PSSNA

Pecan Springs Springdale Hills Neighborhood Association

The Pecan Springs Springdale Hills Neighborhood Association is a major contributor to the development of the Little Walnut Creek Greenbelt.

The Pecan Springs Springdale Hills Neighborhood Association (PSSNA) has been instrumental in advocating for and supporting the creation and development of the Little Walnut Creek Greenbelt. Their dedication to preserving green spaces and promoting community engagement has been essential to making this greenbelt a reality for East Austin residents.

Through their ongoing efforts, PSSNA has worked tirelessly to ensure that the greenbelt serves as a valuable natural resource for the community, providing access to trails, wildlife habitats, and recreational opportunities for neighbors and visitors alike.

We are grateful for their partnership and commitment to environmental stewardship in our neighborhood.
